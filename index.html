Welcome to ReciFly. This web application takes images of a food captured with your computer's camera, recognizes the type of food, stores the name of the food, and allows the user to view recipes that include the foods as ingredients. ReciFly is a next level application. This application can be used globally. Impoverished areas that may not have access to the best food/nutritional resources can scan the foods that they do have and learn of new and innovated ways to receive the proper bodily sustinence. To learn more about how to this application affects other populations, read on.

Urban Application

In more urban areas, ReciFly can be used for people who want to make tasty meals with more healthy ingredients. The application in turn promotes a more healthy lifestyle because people will become more congizant of how healthy ingredients can make tasty meals! What's better than tasty yet nutritous meals?
